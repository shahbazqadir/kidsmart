Return & Refund Policy

7-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with any product, you can return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Eligibility

You can return a product if:

  • Item is unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and all accessories are intact
  • Return request is made within 7 days of delivery
  • No signs of wear or damage (except manufacturer defects)

Non-returnable items:

  • Used or opened products
  • Items without original packaging
  • Products with visible signs of use
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Clearance or final sale items

How to Initiate a Return

Step 1: Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 7 days of delivery
Step 2: Provide order number and reason for return
Step 3: Arrange pickup or send return shipping label
Step 4: We inspect and process refund within 3-5 business days

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • Refund time: 5-10 business days after inspection
  • Free return shipping for defective/damaged items
  • Return shipping cost (Rs. 200-300) deducted for customer-initiated returns

Defective or Damaged Items

  • If item arrives damaged, report within 24 hours
  • Full refund or replacement at no cost
  • We'll arrange pickup of damaged item
  • No need to wait for courier inspection

Exchange Policy

  • Exchange for different size/color is free
  • Price difference waived if upgrading to higher value item
  • Exchange within 7 days of delivery
  • Express exchange available for additional Rs. 200
